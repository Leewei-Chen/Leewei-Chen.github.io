Reimagine Tetrahedron-Robotic Clay Printing

Philadelphia, PA | 2024

Exterior visualization of the twin tower development.

Project Overview

The studio is inspired by the emergence of robotic clay printing, serving as a reinterpretation of Robinson Fredenthal's works. We explore this technique by incorporating clay printing methods alongside studies of curve alterations and color gradients. These effects are achieved through innovative approaches to filling the canister and mixing the clay. Additionally, our final design integrates sound interaction and AI-driven emotional detection, enhancing the overall experience and engagement.
